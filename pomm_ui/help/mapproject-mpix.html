mpix

Output Pixel Size (meters/pixel)

The output map-projected image is resampled to the specified pixel resolution (meters).

  • Example: 6.0

Typical pixel scales for CTX are 5.5 to 6.5 meters. HRSC scales are typically 12.0 or 12.5 pixels, although some collections may be 20, 50, or 100 meters. HiRISE images are typically 0.25 to 2.0m/pxl (1 meter/pixel provides a good balance of fast processing and reduced artifacts).
